Recipe #1

  • Time: 3 hour
  • Tasty: 9/10
  • Serves: 12
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 1 cup
Eggs 2

Step 1:Prepare dough

Step 2:Place milk, eggs, margarine, flour, salt, white sugar, and yeast in the pan of a bread machine in the order recommended by the manufacturer. Select dough cycle; press Start.

Step 3:Roll dough on a lightly floured surface to a 16x21-inch rectangle

Recipe #2

  • Time: 20 min
  • Tasty: 7.5/10
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Milk 1.5 cup
Eggs 1

Step 1:Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.

Step 2:Whisk egg, salt, and pepper together in a medium bowl.

Step 3:Add ground beef and bread crumbs; mix with your hands or a fork until well blended

Step 4:Place patties on the preheated grill. Cover and cook 6 to 8 minutes per side, or to desired doneness. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 160 degrees F (70 degrees C).

Recipe #3

  • Time: 1 hour
  • Tasty: 8/10
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Broccoli 2 cup
Eggs 2

Step 1:Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)

Step 2:Place broccoli and cauliflower in separate saucepans, cover with water, and bring to a boil; cook until tender, 3 to 4 minutes. Drain and arrange in an 11x7-inch baking dish.

Step 3:Beat eggs in a bowl until creamy. Stir in 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese, mayonnaise, and onion; pour over vegetables. Pour melted butter on top and sprinkle with remaining 1/2 cup Cheddar cheese. Scatter crushed croutons evenly on top.

Step 4:Bake in the preheated oven until browned and bubbling, about 40 minutes
